# Welcome to the AI & Humanoid Robotics Textbook

This open-source textbook is designed to be a comprehensive, hands-on guide to the world of physical artificial intelligence and humanoid robotics.

## Our Goal

Our goal is to provide a structured learning path that takes you from the foundational concepts of AI and robotics to advanced topics in humanoid development and conversational AI. This book is for students, hobbyists, and professionals who want to build and program physical, intelligent systems.

## What You'll Learn

This textbook is divided into 6 core modules:

1.  **The Foundation**: Core concepts of AI, robotics, and the Python ecosystem.
2.  **ROS 2**: The Robot Operating System, a fundamental framework for robotics.
3.  **Gazebo**: A powerful 3D robotics simulator.
4.  **NVIDIA Isaac**: Advanced simulation and AI tools for robotics.
5.  **Humanoid Development**: The specifics of building and programming humanoid robots.
6.  **Conversational Robotics/Capstone**: Integrating language models and building a final project.

Navigate through the modules using the sidebar to begin your journey into the exciting world of humanoid robotics!
